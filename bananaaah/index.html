Our story

Made in small batches, the slow way

Bananaaah started in a home kitchen and still runs on the same small-batch habit. We buy the bananas ripe, layer each tub by hand, and let them rest in the fridge so the biscuits soften into the cream. We make every batch to order, so nothing sits around waiting for a buyer.

Nothing is mass-produced and nothing lingers. What we make today goes out fresh, and every tub gets a scatter of Biscoff crumbs before the lid goes on. We build them with biscuits and Lotus Biscoff cookies, and keep it on the less-sweet side so the banana stays the loudest thing in the tub. If a batch isn't right, it doesn't leave the kitchen.

Good to know

How far ahead do I need to order?

About three days. Everything is made to order, so the earlier your form comes in, the safer your slot. Weekends fill up fastest.

Where do you deliver?

We're based in Laguna and cover Sta. Rosa, Cabuyao, Calamba and Los Baños. Outside those areas, message us on Instagram and we'll figure something out. Pickup is always an option too.

How long does it keep?

Kept frozen, it holds for up to a month. For the best flavor and texture, eat it within a week. If you're having it right away, the fridge is fine, no need to freeze at all.

How do I pay?

GCash or GoTyme. The payment details come with your order confirmation after you send the form.

Is it too sweet?

No, and that's deliberate. We aim for not-too-sweet so the banana comes through. Most people tell us it's the reason they can finish a whole tub without feeling sick of it.

What sizes can I get?

The Classic comes in 8oz (₱145) and 12oz (₱210). The other flavors come in one 12oz size at ₱275, and Biscoffanana at ₱295. The 8oz is a good single serving; the 12oz is the one to share, or not.
